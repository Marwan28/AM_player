AM Player Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

AM Player is an on-device media player developed by Marwan Abdelwahab. This policy explains how the current AM Player MVP handles information.

Media access

AM Player requests access to videos and audio stored on your device so it can build your local library and play media you choose. It does not request all-files access and does not request access to your photo library.

Local processing and storage

Media indexing, thumbnails, playback positions, queue state, and app preferences are processed and stored locally on your device. AM Player does not upload your media files or local library metadata.

Data collection and sharing

This MVP does not require an account and does not include advertising or analytics SDKs. It does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

Temporary files

The app may create temporary artwork, thumbnails, and subtitle cache files inside app-controlled storage. These can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Your choices and data retention

You can revoke video or audio access at any time in Android settings. Local playback history and preferences remain until you clear AM Player's app data or uninstall the app.

Children

AM Player does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app processes only the local media that the device user permits it to access.

Changes to this policy

If a future version adds advertising, analytics, cloud features, or other data processing, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before release.
